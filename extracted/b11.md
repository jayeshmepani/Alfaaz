into the quiet corridors of yesters,

becoming just another “was.”

A fracture, yes—
but also a possibility.

a zillion new verses began,
each warblin’ in their own strange meter.

And so I walk,
betwixt opposites,
carrying the unveils,
To revise the narrative
is to write a poem upon the bones of time—
half prose, half poesy,
and wholly unleashed.
